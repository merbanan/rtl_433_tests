# No real .cu8 captures exist for this device anywhere (checked this repo and the
# rtl_433/rtl_433_tests issue trackers). The first 5 codes below are, however, real
# device data: hand-transcribed hex worked examples from the PR's own doc-comment,
# each independently confirmed valid by recomputing its CRC-32 (poly 0x04c11db7,

# that a fabricated example could not satisfy by coincidence.
